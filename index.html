About India

The culture of India is one of the oldest and most diverse in the world,shaped by a thousand of years of history, religious beliefs, traditions and regional variations.

  • India is the birthplace of various religions like Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism. People of various religions stay here with harmony and peace ,respecting each others beliefs.
  • India is known to be a country of numerous of colorful festivals. Some of the major festivals include Diwali,Holi,Eid,Durga puja,Christmas and many more. These festivals often showcase India's unity in diversity.
  • India has the second highest number of languages, 780 out of which 22 are scheduled languages.
  • Ancient temples, palaces, forts and cave paintings display stunning craftmanship and intriciate designs.
  • Indian dance forms have fluid movements and expressiveness with intricate hand gestures.
